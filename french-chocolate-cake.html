Ingredients


    For The Batter:
  • 500 g dark chocolate (70%)
  • 50 g butter
  • 100 g sugar
  • 15 g flour
  • 4 eggs

    • anti stick coat
  • vegetable oil
  • cocoa powder
  • powdered sugar

Method


  1. melt chocolate on low heat
  2. add butter and let melt
  3. seperate egg yolk and egg white, add egg yolk one by one into the butter-chocolate mixture and meanwhile mix egg white until foamy
  4. add sugar to chocolate mixture
  5. add flour to chocolate mixture
  6. let cool down and add egg white
  7. mix oil, sugar, and cocoa powder in seperate bowl and cover cake pan with the sticky mixture.
  8. add cake batter to cake pan and bake for 40 min 150 degree in oven
  9. inside of the cake stays liquidy after bake, dont backe longer than 40 min. let cool and enjoy
