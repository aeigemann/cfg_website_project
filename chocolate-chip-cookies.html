Ingredients


    For The Batter:
  • 100 g dark chocolate (70%)
  • 1 Apple
  • Honey
  • 200 g flour
  • 100 g sugar
  • 100 g butter

Method


  1. cut apple and cook with one spoon of water and honey on medium heat until soft and mash to make puree
  2. while apple-puree cools down, mix flour, sugar, and butter until it forms a nice dough
  3. cut chocolate into small chocolate chips
  4. (alternatively buy grated chocolate)
  5. add apple-puree to dough and mix
  6. add chocolate chips to dough, mix and form 24 spheres
  7. place 12 cookies on oven tray and bake for 15 min at 180 degrees.
  8. repeat for remaining 12 cookies
  9. after baking push spheres flat with a spoon, let cool and enjoy
